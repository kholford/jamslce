Recommended workflow

  1. Review Candidate Summary to confirm priorities.
  2. Use Evaluation System for matrix, intelligence notes, and scoring reference.
  3. Copy eval-form.html to a facility-specific form before each call or tour.
  4. Capture raw data in the facility form, then summarize movement decisions in Decision Log.
  5. Track the military widow / surviving spouse benefit workstream in VA Benefits.
